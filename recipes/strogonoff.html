Strogonoff Recipe

Description

Easy chicken stroganoff - pan-fried chunks of chicken and earthy mushrooms, cooked in a creamy, sightly tangy sauce with Dijon mustard and sour cream. This quick and easy dinner comes together in about 20 minutes. It's rich, velvety comfort food!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • 3 chicken breasts, cut into cubes
  • Garlic
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • Salt
  • Salt to taste
  • Black Pepper
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Onion
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • Mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons of mayonnaise
  • Butter
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • Ketchup
  • 1/2 cup of ketchup
  • Mustard
  • 1/3 cup of mustard
  • Mushroom
  • 1 cup of mushrooms
  • Cream
  • 1 cup of cream
  • Potato Sticks (Potato Straws)
  • Potato sticks to taste
